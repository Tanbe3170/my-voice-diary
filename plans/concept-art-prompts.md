# サバイバルゲーム風コンセプトアート プロンプト集

## 概要

アヌログナトゥスを主人公としたサバイバルゲーム風ストーリーの8シーンプロンプト。
油絵調コンセプトアートとして統一。パレオアートモード改善の検証基準として使用。

## ストーリーライン

1. 医者の子として生まれ、恐竜好きだが医者になるプレッシャーに反抗
2. 事故→タイムリープ→白亜紀へ
3. 病気の恐竜たちの体内に入り治療
4. 体内の病原体（虫歯菌・病気）= 白亜紀風の虫・怪物として戦う
5. 治した恐竜の影を召喚して戦える（Devil May Cry の Devil Trigger 的）
6. 隕石衝突をきっかけに現代へ帰還
7. 学んだ技術と心で本物の医者になる

## 共通設定

- **画風**: Oil painting style, survival game concept art
- **主人公**: Anurognathus（小型翼竜）
- **カラー進行**: 抑圧的暗色 → 原始的暖色 → 爆発的金紫 → 破滅的赤橙 → 希望の金白
- **アスペクト比推奨**: 16:9（コンセプトアート標準）

## Scene 1: The Reluctant Heir / 反抗する跡継ぎ

**物語的意味:** 主人公の出発点。医者への期待と恐竜への情熱の対立。

```
Oil painting style, survival game concept art. A dimly lit doctor's office at twilight. A small Anurognathus pterosaur wearing an oversized white lab coat sits on a massive medical textbook, looking defiantly away from a stethoscope draped over its neck. The walls are covered with dinosaur posters and anatomical charts side by side. A towering shadow of a stern human doctor looms over the tiny pterosaur from behind. Scattered dinosaur figurines and medical instruments on the mahogany desk. Warm amber light from a desk lamp contrasts with cold blue moonlight through the window. Chiaroscuro lighting, Dutch Golden Age palette with deep browns, warm golds, and melancholic blues. Low camera angle looking up at the small pterosaur to emphasize its smallness against the oppressive environment. Cinematic composition with rule of thirds, the pterosaur positioned at the left intersection point.
```

---

## Scene 2: The Accident / 事故と時空の裂け目

**物語的意味:** 転換点。現代と白亜紀が交差する瞬間。

```
Oil painting style, survival game concept art. A dramatic split-screen composition showing a violent temporal rift tearing through reality. On the left side, a modern city street at night with shattered glass and car headlights frozen mid-collision, rain suspended in air. On the right side, a lush Cretaceous jungle with massive ferns, cycads, and volcanic mountains under a primordial orange sky. In the center of the rift, a tiny Anurognathus pterosaur tumbles through the swirling vortex of golden temporal energy, its small wings spread wide, eyes filled with terror and wonder. The rift crackles with electric amber and deep violet energy. Baroque dramatic lighting, Caravaggio-inspired contrast. Color palette transitions from cold urban grays and neon blues on the left to warm prehistoric greens, oranges, and earth tones on the right. Extreme wide shot, symmetrical composition emphasizing the divide between two worlds.
```

---

## Scene 3: Arrival in the Cretaceous / 白亜紀への降臨

**物語的意味:** 新世界への到着。圧倒的スケールと最初の使命の発見。

```
Oil painting style, survival game concept art. A breathtaking panoramic vista of the Late Cretaceous period. A tiny Anurognathus pterosaur perches on a massive fallen log in the foreground, gazing out at an overwhelming prehistoric landscape. Towering conifers and giant ferns frame the scene. In the middle ground, a sick Triceratops lies on its side, its frill discolored with dark purple lesions, breathing heavily with visible pain. Other dinosaurs move in the far background — a herd of Parasaurolophus crossing a misty river, pteranodons soaring overhead. Volumetric god rays pierce through the canopy, creating pools of golden light on the forest floor. Active volcanoes glow on the distant horizon under a massive amber sky with prehistoric cloud formations. Hudson River School painting aesthetic combined with survival game environmental storytelling. Rich palette of deep emerald greens, volcanic oranges, misty lavenders, and warm earth tones. Wide establishing shot with strong foreground-middleground-background layering.
```

---

## Scene 4: Entering the Body / 体内への侵入

**物語的意味:** ダンジョン突入。医療が冒険になる瞬間。

```
Oil painting style, survival game concept art. A surreal interior landscape depicting the inside of a dinosaur's body reimagined as a vast Cretaceous cavern system. The small Anurognathus pterosaur flies through an enormous blood vessel rendered as a bioluminescent cave tunnel with walls of living red-orange tissue that pulse with a heartbeat glow. Red blood cells reimagined as floating crimson boulders drift through the space. White blood cells appear as primitive armored organisms resembling ancient crustaceans with glowing blue cores, patrolling the passages. The tunnel ahead splits into multiple branching paths like a dungeon map, with each path glowing a different ominous color — sickly green for infection, deep purple for disease. Stalactites of crystallized minerals hang from above. A game-like HUD overlay subtly etched into the painting's border suggests health bars and a minimap. Dramatic bioluminescent lighting in deep crimsons, electric blues, and amber. Fantastical anatomical illustration meets survival horror game aesthetic. First-person perspective looking over the shoulder of the flying Anurognathus into the vast organic cavern ahead.
```

---

## Scene 5: Battle Against Disease / 病魔との戦い

**物語的意味:** クライマックスバトル。医療知識が武器になる。

```
Oil painting style, survival game concept art. An epic battle scene inside a dinosaur's body. The tiny but fierce Anurognathus pterosaur wields a glowing scalpel-shaped energy blade, diving in combat against monstrous disease creatures. The main enemy is a colossal cavity bug — a grotesque beetle-like monster with drill mandibles made of corroded tooth enamel, its carapace covered in bacterial colonies that glow sickly yellow-green. Smaller infection parasites swarm like Cretaceous-era insects with segmented bodies and venomous stingers. The battlefield is a massive tooth interior reimagined as a crumbling cathedral of ivory and dentin, with stalactite-like structures being devoured by the bugs. The Anurognathus unleashes a beam of healing light from a small medical kit strapped to its chest. Splashes of antibacterial energy explode in brilliant white and gold against the dark, corrupted tissue. Action-packed diagonal composition with strong motion lines. Palette of corrupted sickly greens and yellows against heroic whites and golds. Dramatic backlighting creating rim light on the protagonist. Dynamic low-angle shot emphasizing the scale difference between the tiny hero and the massive disease boss.
```

## Scene 6: Devil Trigger — Summoning Allies / 魔人化：恐竜召喚

**物語的意味:** 覚醒。治した恐竜たちとの絆が力になる。

```
Oil painting style, survival game concept art. The climactic Devil Trigger transformation scene. The small Anurognathus pterosaur hovers at the center of the composition, wings fully spread, eyes blazing with intense white-gold light, body surrounded by crackling healing energy. Behind and around the tiny pterosaur, three massive dinosaur shadows materialize as towering spectral silhouettes — a Triceratops shadow charges from the left with luminous horns, a Tyrannosaurus Rex shadow roars from the right with jaws of blue ghostly fire, and an Ankylosaurus shadow rises from below with its tail club radiating shockwaves. The shadows are rendered as dark indigo and deep purple translucent apparitions with glowing anatomical details — bones, circulatory systems, and healed scars visible within their spectral forms like X-ray images. The ground beneath cracks and erupts with golden light. Enemy disease creatures cower and disintegrate before the overwhelming presence. Renaissance master painting composition with the protagonist as the central divine figure, surrounded by the trinity of dinosaur guardians in a triangular arrangement. Palette of deep royal purples, midnight blues, spectral indigos against explosive golds, whites, and healing greens. Extreme dramatic chiaroscuro, Rembrandt-meets-Capcom aesthetic. Slight low-angle heroic shot.
```

## Scene 7: The Asteroid Impact / 小惑星衝突と帰還

**物語的意味:** 別れと帰還。恐竜たちの犠牲と主人公の再出発。

```
Oil painting style, survival game concept art. The catastrophic asteroid impact scene marking the end of the Cretaceous period. An enormous fireball dominates the upper portion of the canvas, the Chicxulub asteroid streaking across a blood-red sky leaving a trail of superheated debris. The horizon line erupts with a blinding white wall of destruction advancing rapidly. In the foreground, the small Anurognathus pterosaur is shielded within a protective circle formed by its dinosaur allies — the Triceratops lowers its frill as a shield, the Tyrannosaurus stands tall roaring defiantly at the sky, the Ankylosaurus hunkers down creating a barrier. Their bodies begin to glow and dissolve into golden light particles as they sacrifice their physical forms to protect the protagonist. A temporal rift identical to Scene 2 tears open behind the Anurognathus, pulling it backward toward the modern era. The pterosaur reaches one small wing toward its dissolving dinosaur friends in a heartbreaking farewell gesture. J.M.W. Turner-inspired apocalyptic sky with extreme atmospheric effects. Palette of catastrophic oranges, apocalyptic reds, sacrificial golds, and sorrowful deep blues. Sweeping cinematic wide shot with strong diagonal energy from upper right to lower left. Emotional peak of the entire series.
```

## Scene 8: The Real Doctor / 本物の医者になる

**物語的意味:** エピローグ。Scene 1との対比。成長と和解。

```
Oil painting style, survival game concept art. The epilogue scene — a triumphant return to the modern world. A bright, warm modern hospital room bathed in morning sunlight streaming through large windows. The Anurognathus pterosaur now confidently wears a perfectly fitted white doctor's coat with a stethoscope, standing on a medical cart beside a young dinosaur patient (a small sick Compsognathus) lying in a hospital bed, gently examining it with genuine care and expertise. Medical instruments are arranged neatly — but mixed among them are Cretaceous-era healing crystals and organic tools from the pterosaur's journey. On the wall behind, very faintly visible as ghostly impressions in the morning light, are the spectral silhouettes of the Triceratops, T-Rex, and Ankylosaurus — guardian spirits watching over their old friend, smiling. The same desk from Scene 1 is visible in the corner, but now the dinosaur figurines and medical books coexist harmoniously, no longer in conflict. A framed photo on the desk shows the Cretaceous landscape. Warm Vermeer-inspired interior lighting with soft golden tones dominating. Palette of hopeful warm golds, clean whites, gentle sky blues, and subtle spectral purples for the dinosaur ghosts. Peaceful, resolved composition mirroring Scene 1 but transformed — same elements, completely different emotional tone. Medium shot at eye level with the pterosaur, intimate and personal. The final image in the series, conveying completion, growth, and earned peace.
```
